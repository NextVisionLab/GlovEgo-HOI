GlovEgo-Net

Bridging the Synthetic-to-Real Gap for Industrial Egocentric Human–Object Interaction

VISAPP @ VISIGRAPP 2026

GitHub Repository Paper (PDF)

Abstract

GlovEgo-Net addresses the problem of Egocentric Human–Object Interaction (EHOI) detection in industrial environments. The proposed framework mitigates the synthetic-to-real domain gap by combining large-scale photorealistic synthetic data with diffusion-augmented real-world imagery. Our approach explicitly models hand–object interactions, contact states, 21-keypoint hand pose, and Personal Protective Equipment (PPE), with a particular focus on industrial work gloves.

GlovEgo-HOI Dataset

The GlovEgo-HOI dataset is a benchmark for industrial EHOI detection, composed of 28,738 annotated images integrating synthetic and real data.

GlovEgo-HOI-Synth – Photorealistic synthetic data generated with Unity Perception

GlovEgo-HOI-Real – Real images augmented via FLUX.1 Diffusion

Experimental Results

GlovEgo-Net consistently outperforms real-only baselines, demonstrating the effectiveness of synthetic pre-training for industrial EHOI detection.
